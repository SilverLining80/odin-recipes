Gnocchi

Ingredients

  • 2 potatoes, peeled
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil, add ingredients.
  2. Combine 1 cup mashed potato, flour, and egg in a large bowl.
  3. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Drop in gnocchi and cook for 3 to 5 minutes.
